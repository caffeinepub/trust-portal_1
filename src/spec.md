# Specification

## Summary
**Goal:** Remove Leadership Team section from About page and clear placeholder media from Gallery page.

**Planned changes:**
- Remove Leadership Team section from About page
- Remove all placeholder photos from Gallery photo tab
- Remove all placeholder videos from Gallery video tab
